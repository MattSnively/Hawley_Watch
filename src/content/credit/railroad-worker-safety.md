After the devastating Norfolk Southern train derailment in East Palestine, Ohio (February 2023), Hawley co-sponsored the bipartisan Railway Safety Act with senators from both parties.

He publicly advocated for the bill, maintained consistent messaging about railroad safety, and backed his words with legislative action. This is one of his genuinely consistent positions.

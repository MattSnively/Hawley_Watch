status: "Introduced"
---

Hawley has been one of the most vocal proponents of banning congressional stock trading. His PELOSI Act (Preventing Elected Leaders from Owning Securities and Investments Act) would prohibit members of Congress and their spouses from holding individual stocks.

This is one of Hawley's genuinely consistent positions — his rhetoric matches his legislative action, and he has maintained this stance over multiple years.

## PAC Contributions

### Hawley's Stance on Corporate PACs
In April 2021, Hawley's campaign announced it would **stop accepting corporate PAC money** after major corporations criticized Georgia voting laws.

## Small Dollar Donations

Hawley relies heavily on small donors:

- Small donations don't require name disclosure
- His fundraising spiked after January 6, 2021

## Key Narratives for Your Site

### 1. "Populist" Who Takes Wall Street Money
Despite anti-Big Tech, anti-elite rhetoric:
- **$833,820** from Securities & Investment industry
- **$738,153** from Real Estate industry
- Top donor is Jones Financial (Edward Jones parent company)

### 2. Oil & Gas Connections
- **$292,886** from Oil & Gas industry
- Koch-affiliated Americans for Prosperity spent $4M to elect him in 2018

### 3. The McConnell Connection
- Senate Leadership Fund spent **$21.2 million** to elect Hawley in 2018
- Hawley later introduced bill to overturn Citizens United
- McConnell publicly criticized him, noting SLF support

### 4. Post-January 6 Fundraising Surge
- Despite corporate backlash after his fist-pump photo
- Small-dollar donations increased significantly
- Most major corporate PACs stopped giving

### 5. 2024: Outraised But Still Won
- Kunce raised nearly 2x what Hawley raised
- Hawley won by 14 points anyway
- Shows fundraising isn't everything in red Missouri

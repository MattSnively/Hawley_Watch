Hawley has introduced and co-sponsored multiple pieces of legislation aimed at breaking up or regulating dominant tech companies like Google, Amazon, Facebook/Meta, and Apple.

This is a bipartisan issue where Hawley's rhetoric and legislative record align. He has consistently pushed for antitrust action against Big Tech.
